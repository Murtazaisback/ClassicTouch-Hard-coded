Tailored Solutions

At Classic Touch, we understand that a standard solution rarely creates a standout brand. With 4000+ completed projects, we’ve built our reputation on the promise of complete customization.

  • Bespoke Design: We don’t use templates. Every exhibition stand, retail fixture, or branding element is designed from the ground up to fit your exact dimensions, functional requirements, and budget.

  • Material Selection: We guide you in selecting the ideal wood type, finish, and complementary materials to ensure aesthetic impact and lasting durability.

  • Local Focus: Our tailored approach prioritizes the needs of local businesses, ensuring your physical brand presence resonates directly with your specific market and venue.

The Quality of Experience: Hands-On Since 2014.

The “Classic Touch” difference lies in the hands-on expertise of founder Nasir ALI and our dedicated team. Our process balances time-honored woodworking techniques with modern manufacturing efficiency.

  • Precision and Detail: Every joint, cut, and finish is executed with meticulous attention to detail, transforming raw materials into structurally sound and beautiful brand assets.

  • Quality Control: Unlike mass-production facilities, every stage of manufacturing—from initial material preparation to final assembly—is subject to rigorous quality checks to ensure flawlessness.

  • Mastery of Materials: Our expertise covers a wide range of materials, including solid wood, engineered panels, and bespoke laminates, allowing us to deliver complex, multi-textured displays that perform under pressure.

Commitment to Your Success

For us, a project is not complete until it actively contributes to your success. Our commitment is rooted in the long-term relationships we build with our 178+ clients and our drive to maximize your investment.

  • Reliability & Deadlines: We are dedicated to on-time, on-budget delivery, ensuring your exhibition stand is ready well before opening day or your new retail fit-out is installed with minimal disruption.

  • Post-Project Support: Our service extends to installation, tear-down (for stands), and any necessary follow-up support to maintain the integrity and appearance of your woodwork.

  • Future-Proofing: We design for durability and reusability, helping you maximize the lifespan of your displays and brand elements for long-term marketing value.
